American Civil War

The American Civil War, fought from 1861 to 1865, was a conflict between the Northern states (Union) and the Southern states (Confederacy) over issues such as slavery and states rights. The war resulted in the Union's victory, the abolition of slavery, Reconstruction and Civil Rights.

The Emancipation Proclamation in 1863

The Middle Ages 5th - 15th century

The Middle Ages was a period marked by feudalism, the rise of kingdoms, and the spread of Christianity across Europe. It saw key historical events such as the Black Death and the Crusades.

Events:

Black death

The Black Death was a devastating plague that swept through Europe between 1347 and 1351
Black Death took more than 20 million lives in Europe.

Crusades

The Crusades were a series of religious wars initiated by European Christians between the 1095 and 1291 to reclaim the Holy Land from Muslim control.
